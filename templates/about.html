How Our System Works

We utilize cutting-edge Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to transform and enhance your Human Resources decision-making process.

The Technology Under the Hood

spaCy for Parsing

We use **spaCy**, an industrial-strength NLP library, to efficiently and accurately extract key entities like skills, education, experience, and contact information from unstructured resume and JD files.

Semantic Matching (BERT/TF-IDF)

For matching, we leverage advanced models like **BERT** (or traditional **TF-IDF**) to understand the meaning and context of words, calculating semantic similarity between the job requirements and candidate profiles.

Data-Driven Ranking

The system generates a quantitative **Match Score** and ranks candidates based on skill overlap, experience relevance, and education fit, providing HR with objective data for fast shortlisting.
